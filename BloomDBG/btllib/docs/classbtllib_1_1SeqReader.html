Detailed Description

Read a FASTA, FASTQ, SAM, or GFA2 file. Capable of reading gzipped (.gz), bzipped (.bz2), xzipped (.xz), zipped (.zip), 7zipped (.7z), BAM (.bam) and CRAM (.cram), and URL (http://, https://, ftp://) files. Threadsafe.
